What We Offer

End-to-End Agri-Input Regulatory Services

We handle the complexity of central and state-level compliance so you can focus on innovation and market growth.

Pesticide Registration (CIB&RC)

Full professional support for getting your products registered under the Insecticides Act.

  • Sections 9(3) and 9(4) for Chemical & Biopesticides.
  • Import and Export Permissions.
  • Complete CIB&RC submission and approval support.

Fertilizer & Bio-stimulant Compliance (FCO)

Expert guidance for all product categories under the Fertilizer Control Order (FCO).

  • Permanent Registration of Bio-stimulants (End-to-end support).
  • Registration of Organic, Inorganic, and Specialty Fertilizers.
  • Query resolution and remark compliance assistance.

Pan-India State Permissions & Licenses

Secure all necessary operating permissions across various state regulatory bodies.

  • Sales Permissions for all inputs (Fertilizers, Pesticides, Bio-stimulants) in all Indian states.
  • Documentation, submission, and follow-up at the state level.
  • Support for obtaining Manufacturing Licenses.

Technical & Scientific Advisory
